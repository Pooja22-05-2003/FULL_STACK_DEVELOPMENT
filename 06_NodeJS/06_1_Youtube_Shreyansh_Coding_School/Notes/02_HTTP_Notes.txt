Q1  What is protocol?
-> Protocol means rules
-> Internet par kuch bhi karne ke liye rules banaye gye hai, unke dwaara jinhone internet bananaya hai.Ab un rules ko follow krna bhot zaruri hai , aur ye rules follow ho isliye ye rules aap ke operating system ke software mei preinstalled ate hai.

-> Yahi protocol hai ya rule hai jisko follow kre bina aap internet pe naa hi kuch bhej skte ho , naa hi kuch manga skte ho.
